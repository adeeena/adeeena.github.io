SIt’s been officially deduced: trans folks are almost all DJs, gay men are either artists or photographers, and lesbians are all Subaru-driving carpenters. Following this airtight logic, pansexuals must be plant-collecting, DIY-project-obsessed scavengers. Naturally, this realization hit me while I was sitting in a living room decorated like a literal jungle, surrounded by the remnants of her own DIY experiments.

As for Brandon! The name sounds exactly like the cliché, basic USA starter pack. He’s the guy from high school who sat in the back row for four years, blissfully unaware of anything while being elbow-deep in his own nose.
